Asia

Asia's travel history spans millennia, showcasing ancient marvels like Angkor Wat in Cambodia, a symbol of Khmer architecture and spirituality. The Great Wall of China stands as a testament to centuries of engineering and fortification, while India's Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, remains an enduring symbol of love and architectural excellence. These landmarks, among many others, weave together a narrative of cultural richness, innovation, and the enduring legacy of civilizations across Asia.
